## Relative Macro Transmission Interpretation

This module compares two selected macro or market surfaces to identify divergence, alignment, and structural transmission states.

It does not provide directional signals. It helps frame relative system conditions.

---

#### Core Transformations

### Difference

Measures the absolute spread between two selected series.

Best used for:

- Interest rate differentials
- Yield spreads
- External balance divergence
- Relative sovereign pricing

A widening spread may indicate strengthening divergence.

---

### Ratio

Measures proportional relationship between two selected series.

Best used for:

- Relative scale comparison
- Structural proportionality
- Long-cycle relationship framing

Useful where absolute values matter less than proportional dominance.

---

### Relative %

Measures percentage outperformance or underperformance.

Best used for:

- Relative wealth comparisons
- Equity market outperformance
- Cross-market return surfaces

Helps assess comparative strength across systems.

---

### Relative Z-Score

Standardises the current relationship relative to its own history.

Best used for:

- Regime detection
- Structural divergence
- Historical context framing

Higher absolute values suggest movement away from historical norms.

---

### Rolling Correlation

Shows how the relationship between two selected surfaces changes over time.

Best used for:

- Decoupling detection
- Re-coupling analysis
- Commodity and FX transmission

A falling rolling correlation may indicate structural breakdown.

---

#### Structural States

| State | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| **Aligned** | Relationship remains broadly within historical range |
| **Mild Divergence** | Early movement away from normal conditions |
| **Material Divergence** | Clear visible separation from prior structure |
| **Regime Shift** | Relationship has moved materially outside prior range |
| **Re-coupling** | Relationship is moving back toward historical alignment |

---

#### Important Note

Relative inflation and deflation must be interpreted carefully.

A currency may weaken on an absolute basis while strengthening on a relative basis if the opposing system is deteriorating faster.

This module supports that comparative framing.

---

#### Summary Insight

Relative Macro Transmission helps identify:

- whether divergence is expanding
- whether a prior regime is breaking
- whether structural alignment is returning

It is designed for context navigation, not prediction.
