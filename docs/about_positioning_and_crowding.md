## Positioning & Crowding

This module provides a weekly lens into leveraged futures positioning across core FX, rates, equity, and volatility markets.

It helps identify where positioning is becoming crowded, where participants are shifting exposure, and whether current market structure may require additional caution before execution.

The tool focuses on:

- Leveraged long versus short positioning
- Position concentration and crowding
- Historical percentile extremes
- Positioning turns and regime shifts
- Weekly positioning versus market price context

##### ⚠️ This module is observational and gatekeeping-focused.

It does not determine direction by itself.

It is used to assess whether positioning supports, contradicts, or warns against an existing macro view.

For broader structural macro analysis, use Relative Macro Transmission.
For execution timing and volatility assessment, refer to Market & Volatility Scanner.
