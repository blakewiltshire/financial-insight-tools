
# ℹ️ Help: How to read these summaries

- **Average Spread Ratio**: Mean ratio of long/short asset prices over the period.

- **Volatility (Std Dev)**: Standard deviation of the spread ratio, showing historical variability.

- **Deviation from Mean (Z-Score)**: How far the current ratio is from its historical mean in standard deviation terms.

- **Support/Resistance Levels**: Historically significant levels where the spread ratio has tended to reverse.

- **Correlation (Long vs Short)**: Measures how closely the two assets move together.
    - Close to **+1**: Strong positive relationship.
    - Close to **0**: Weak or no relationship.
    - Close to **-1**: Strong inverse relationship.

- **Observed Movement**: Actual percentage change in spread over the selected period.

- **Expected Change**: The model’s anticipated percentage change based on historical returns.

- **Drift**: The difference between actual and expected performance, indicating unexpected divergence.
