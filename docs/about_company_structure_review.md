## Company Structure Review

The Company Structure Review module provides a structured framework for comparing companies across valuation, growth, profitability, and market scepticism metrics.

Rather than focusing on price movements or trade timing, the module examines how companies are positioned relative to their peer groups and how the market appears to be assessing those characteristics.

The tool focuses on:

- Relative valuation multiples across peer groups
- Revenue growth and business expansion profiles
- Operating profitability and efficiency measures
- Market scepticism through short-interest observations
- Peer-group averages, medians, rankings, and structural differences
- Observation-ready context for downstream AI review and analysis

### Structural Review Rather Than Prediction

Valuation metrics are often discussed as signals of whether a company appears "cheap" or "expensive." This module approaches the subject differently.

The purpose is not to determine whether a company should rise or fall in value. Instead, the module helps surface what the market currently appears prepared to pay relative to a company's growth profile, profitability characteristics, and peer-group context.

By comparing individual companies against peer averages and medians, users can identify where market expectations differ across a selected group without assuming those expectations are correct or incorrect.

### Available Review Categories

The module currently supports:

- Equities — Magnificent Seven
- Equities — Sector Constituents
- User-defined company comparison groups

Users may also upload their own peer groups for sector-specific, thematic, or company-specific reviews.

### Typical Areas of Exploration

Examples may include:

- Comparing premium and volume manufacturers within the automotive sector
- Reviewing semiconductor companies with differing growth profiles
- Comparing software businesses across profitability and valuation structures
- Exploring how market scepticism varies across a selected peer group

The purpose is to support structured comparison and contextual review rather than directional forecasting.

##### ⚠️ No Investment Advice or Recommendations

This module does not provide investment advice, valuation opinions, fair value estimates, trade signals, portfolio recommendations, or target prices.

All outputs are observational and intended to support structured review, interpretation, and critical thinking. Any decisions, assumptions, or actions taken remain the responsibility of the end user.
