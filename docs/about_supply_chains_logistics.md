This module explores macro-level signals related to supply chain health, logistics infrastructure, and distribution efficiency.
It highlights bottlenecks, freight conditions, and transport-linked economic indicators that affect production, inventory, and trade flows.

Rather than diagnosing firm-level disruptions or modelling operational logistics, the module presents a structured lens on systemic
frictions, delivery performance, and supply-side resilience across sectors and regions.

It integrates with the broader Financial Insight Tools system and applies in contexts with reliable freight, inventory,
or logistics sentiment data.

---

- **Bottleneck Framing:** Highlights stress in supply chains via delivery delays, shipping activity, and inventory swings.
- **Logistics System Signals:** Surfaces freight rates, transport costs, and logistics health indicators.
- **Real-Economy Linkages:** Frames how logistical disruptions ripple into production, inflation, and trade metrics.
- **Modular Integration:** Compatible with other macro modules and supports structured AI or diagnostic overlay.
