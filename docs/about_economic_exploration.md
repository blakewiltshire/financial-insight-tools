## Economic Exploration

The **Economic Exploration** module provides structured access to macroeconomic indicators across regions, subregions, and countries. It enables comparative and country-specific insight across a wide range of themes, including growth, inflation, labour dynamics, and financial conditions.

This launcher serves as the central gateway for d macroeconomic diagnostics:

- Navigate region → subregion → country dashboards
- Explore GDP, inflation, employment, and 20+ thematic groupings
- Use global overlays to assess systemic context and comparative signals
- Launch modular, country-specific dashboards from a unified interface

### ⚠️ No forecasts, recommendations, or policy advice are provided
This module facilitates structured exploration of economic conditions. Interpretive analysis, evaluation, and application are the responsibility of the end user. No predictive modelling, financial recommendations, or advisory content is included.
