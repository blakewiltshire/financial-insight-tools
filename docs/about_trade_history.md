The **Trade History & Strategy** module provides a structured review of executed trades and investment decisions. It validates uploaded trade logs for structural integrity and logical coherence, including checks on entry/exit dates, direction, position sizing, and realised P&L alignment.

This tool applies a system-based lens to assess historical performance ‚Äî supporting workflows where behavioural diagnostics, execution review, and allocation outcomes are central to the decision-making process.

Common uses include:

- Reviewing recorded trade activity for structural and logical consistency  
- Identifying gaps between intended strategy and actual execution  
- Analysing realised returns, directional outcomes, and capital deployment  
- Normalising historical data for export, reflection, or downstream analysis  

---

### Key Functions

- **Status Classification**: Flags trades as open or closed based on exit data  
- **P&L Consistency Check**: Verifies declared vs system-calculated realised performance  
- **Structural Validation**: Detects missing fields, invalid entries, or timing inconsistencies  
- **Output Ready**: Cleaned data is formatted for further review or integration
