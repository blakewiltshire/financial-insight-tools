This module examines central banking signals, monetary aggregates, and the broader liquidity landscape.
It integrates interest rate paths, base money trends, and institutional balance sheet dynamics to assess
policy direction and monetary pressure across macroeconomic regimes.

Rather than asserting causal outcomes or recommending policy paths, the module highlights system-level shifts
in monetary conditions and the resulting market liquidity or credit effects.

It supports comparative framing of monetary strategy across countries or regions with valid monetary data and
integrates with the wider Financial Insight Tools decision framework.

---

- **Interest Rate Framing**: Surfaces benchmark rates, yield signals, and rate environment shifts.
- **Liquidity & Money Supply Context**: Tracks M1/M2 aggregates, credit availability, and central bank asset flows.
- **Policy Transmission Layers**: Includes indicators related to lending standards, reserve ratios, and policy rates.
- **Modular Integration**: Structured for compatibility with observation tools, macro diagnostics, and AI augmentation.
