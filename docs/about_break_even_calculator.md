## Break-Even Distance Calculator

The **Break-Even Distance Calculator** estimates the minimum price movement required for a trade to cover its costs and become profitable.

This includes:
- Entry price
- Commission or spread costs
- Slippage assumptions
- Platform or fixed fees

It helps answer the question:

> “How far does the asset price need to move before I break even?”

This tool is useful for refining trade planning logic within the **Trade Structuring & Risk Planning** suite.

⚠️ No advice is given — this is a structural lens to evaluate pricing and exposure alignment.
