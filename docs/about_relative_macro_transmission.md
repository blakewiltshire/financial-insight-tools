## Relative Macro Transmission

This module provides a structured lens into cross-system differentials between macro and market surfaces.

It helps surface how relative conditions between countries, markets, and policy environments evolve across time, allowing users to assess transmission pressure, divergence, and re-coupling behaviour.

The tool focuses on:

- Relative growth and macro differentials
- Interest rate spreads and carry conditions
- External balance and capital flow divergence
- Currency and commodity transmission relationships
- Sovereign versus equity divergence
- Relative wealth and cross-market positioning

##### ⚠️ This module is comparative by design.

It is intended to support exogenous system analysis and structural interpretation.

For country-specific endogenous conditions, use Economic Exploration.
For execution timing and volatility structure, refer to Market & Volatility Scanner.
