## Financial Insight Tools (FIT)

**Financial Insight Tools (FIT)** is a modular decision-support environment for building structured investigations across financial markets, macroeconomics, and related analytical domains.

Investigations combine market observations, economic signals, company analysis, supporting evidence, and human observations within a connected analytical workflow. Decision Support Snapshots, Research Notes, and structured observations preserve context as investigations evolve, allowing evidence and reasoning to remain connected over time.

The platform is used to:Core investigation environments include:
- Economic exploration and macroeconomic analysis
- Company and market structure review
- Cross-market and thematic relationship analysis
- Trade and portfolio structuring
- Observation capture and investigation management
- AI-assisted investigation through structured evidence bundles

AI contributes by reviewing structured investigations, exploring alternative perspectives, synthesising evidence, and identifying uncertainty. Interpretation and decision-making remain with the user.

### ⚠️ No investment advice or recommendations are provided

**FIT** provides analytical structure, contextual framing, and decision-support workflows. It does not provide trading, investment, financial, legal, tax, or policy advice.
