**Financial Insight Tools (FIT)** is a modular decision-support environment designed to support
structured exploration of markets, economic signals, and analytical workflows.

The platform is used to:
- Structure disciplined trading and investment workflows
- Explore economic signals and cross-market relationships
- Track portfolio performance and post-trade outcomes
- Package insights for AI integration and analytical review

FIT provides analytical structure and contextual framing. It does not provide trading, investment, or policy advice.
