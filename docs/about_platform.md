## Financial Insight Tools (FIT)

**Financial Insight Tools (FIT)** is a modular investigation platform for exploring financial markets, macroeconomics, companies, and the relationships that connect them.

Investigations combine market observations, economic signals, company analysis, supporting evidence, and human observations within a connected workflow. Decision Support Snapshots, Research Notes, and structured observations preserve continuity as investigations develop, allowing evidence, assumptions, and reasoning to remain connected over time.

Individual modules can be used independently to answer specific questions or combined into larger investigations as understanding evolves. Once sufficient context has been assembled, AI can review the investigation, explore alternative perspectives, identify uncertainty, and contribute to structured interpretation.

Interpretation and decision-making remain with the user.

**Core Investigation Environments**
- Economic exploration and macroeconomic analysis
- Relationship expansion and candidate asset organisation
- Cross-market and intermarket analysis
- Company and market structure review
- Trade and portfolio structuring
- Observation management and AI-assisted investigation

### ⚠️ No investment advice or recommendations are provided

**Financial Insight Tools** provides structured investigation, contextual framing, and decision-support workflows. It does not provide trading, investment, financial, legal, tax, or policy advice.
