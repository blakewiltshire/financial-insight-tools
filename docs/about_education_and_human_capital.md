This module explores structured signals related to national education systems and the development of human capital.
It covers investment in education, enrolment across academic levels, and skill formation within the labour force.

Rather than evaluating education policy or outcomes, the module frames macroeconomic signals that reflect
workforce readiness, socio-economic mobility, and productivity capacity.

It integrates with the broader Financial Insight Tools ecosystem and is compatible with cross-country comparisons
where education and human capital data are available.

---

- **Education Investment:** Tracks government and private expenditure on education, including GDP share and per capita metrics.  
- **Skill Formation Signals:** Highlights educational attainment, training access, and workforce skill alignment.  
- **Human Capital Dynamics:** Supports analysis of long-term productivity, income mobility, and economic resilience.  
- **Modular Integration:** Aligns with labour, innovation, and social impact modules in structured analysis workflows.
