This module presents structured insights into currency valuation trends and exchange rate dynamics.
It includes bilateral FX rates, nominal and real effective exchange rate indices (NEER/REER), and
optionally, volatility signals or policy-linked FX sensitivities.

Rather than speculating on currency direction or trade strategies, the module frames macro-level
currency movements in terms of competitiveness, policy alignment, and external balance pressures.

It integrates seamlessly with other modules in the Financial Insight Tools suite and applies to any country
with sufficient foreign exchange and macro-financial data.

---

- **FX Signal Framing:** Surfaces trends in nominal and real exchange rates and currency stability regimes.  
- **Competitiveness & Policy Context:** Enables comparative framing of monetary conditions and external sector performance.  
- **Volatility & Exposure:** Supports diagnostic overlays related to capital flows, hedging pressures, or FX-linked fragility.  
- **Modular Integration:** Aligns with trade, monetary policy, and global macro themes for triangulated insight.
