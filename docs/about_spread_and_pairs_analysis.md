## Pairs trading and spread-based strategies

This module supports evaluation of **pairs trading and spread-based strategies** by analysing
the historical relationship between two assets.

Key Analytical Focus:
- Spread Ratio: Relationship between two assets’ prices over time.
- Z-Score Deviation: Detects divergence from mean, useful for mean reversion strategies.
- Support/Resistance Levels: Historical reversal points in spread behaviour.
- Correlation: Validates whether assets behave in a complementary or inverse fashion.

Trade Application:
This tool supports assessment for strategies such as:
- Pairs Trading (Mean Reversion)
- Pairs Trading (Beta Hedging)
- Multi-Leg Spread** constructions
- Relative Strength Positioning
