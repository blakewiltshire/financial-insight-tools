## Market Structure Review

The Market Structure Review module provides a structured framework for reviewing ownership, float, supply events, institutional participation, and index-related context around selected assets.

Rather than focusing on price movement or trade timing, the module examines how an asset is owned, supplied, and participated in across the market.

It is designed to support structured observation, comparison, and downstream AI review without providing trading, investment, IPO participation, valuation, or portfolio recommendations.

##### ⚠️ No Investment Advice or Recommendations

This module does not provide investment advice, IPO participation recommendations, trade signals, valuation opinions, portfolio guidance, target prices, or forecasts.

All outputs are observational and intended to support structured review, interpretation, and critical thinking.

Ownership structures, float characteristics, supply events, institutional participation, and index eligibility may influence how market participants interpret an asset, but they do not determine future outcomes.

Any decisions, assumptions, or actions taken remain the responsibility of the end user.
