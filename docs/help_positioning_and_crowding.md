## Positioning & Crowding Interpretation

This module helps assess how leveraged participants are positioned across major futures markets.

It is designed to support timing, crowding awareness, and structural caution.

It does not provide standalone directional signals.

---

#### Net Positioning

Net Positioning is calculated as:

**Leveraged Long Positions − Leveraged Short Positions**

This helps show whether leveraged participants are structurally positioned net long or net short.

- Positive values suggest net long positioning
- Negative values suggest net short positioning

---

#### Net Position Share (%)

This is calculated as:

**Long Position Share (%) − Short Position Share (%)**

It helps normalise positioning relative to total market participation.

This is often more useful than absolute contracts alone.

---

#### Positioning Percentile

Percentile shows where the current positioning sits relative to its recent historical range.

Examples:

- **90th percentile+** → positioning is historically stretched
- **50th percentile** → positioning is near normal range
- **10th percentile or lower** → positioning is historically compressed

Extreme percentiles often require caution.

---

#### Positioning Turn

A positioning turn occurs when leveraged positioning shifts from:

- net short → net long
- net long → net short

This does not confirm direction, but often signals a meaningful structural change in participation.

It is especially useful for timing.

---

#### Structural States

| State | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| **Crowded Long** | Positioning is heavily skewed net long |
| **Crowded Short** | Positioning is heavily skewed net short |
| **Moving Towards Extreme** | Positioning is building toward crowding |
| **Within Historical Range** | Positioning remains broadly normal |

---

#### Important Note

Crowded positioning can persist longer than expected.

This module should not be used to “fade” positioning automatically.

Instead, it helps answer:

- Is the move already crowded?
- Is positioning confirming or contradicting the macro view?
- Is timing becoming stretched?

---

#### Summary Insight

Positioning & Crowding acts as a final structural gate before execution.

It helps determine whether the market is early, late, crowded, or transitioning.
