## Observation & AI Export

**Observation & AI Export** Export brings together structured investigations assembled across Financial Insight Tools. Decision Support Snapshots, observations, research notes, and contextual metadata are consolidated into investigation bundles that preserve evidence and reasoning throughout the analytical process.

Investigations remain connected as they evolve, allowing observations to be revisited, expanded, and reviewed through different analytical perspectives.

---
## Investigation Workspace

- **Snapshot Browser:** Review Decision Support Snapshots captured across investigation modules.
- **Observation Browser:** Browse structured observations and supporting reasoning.
- **Research Notes:** Preserve longer-form investigation notes and developing ideas.
- **Build Investigation Bundle:** Assemble snapshots, observations, metadata, and supporting context into portable investigation bundles.
- **AI Personas & Investigation:** Load AI personas, generate investigation prompts, and review structured investigations.
- **BLoad & Restore:** Restore previous investigations and continue analytical workflows.

---

## System Role

Observation & AI Export acts as the investigation layer within Financial Insight Tools, connecting analytical modules with AI-assisted interpretation while preserving context throughout the workflow.

Investigation bundles preserve:

- Structured evidence
- Human observations
- Investigation context
- Cross-module relationships
- Vendor-neutral AI portability

Bundles are intentionally model-agnostic and designed for use with external AI systems without requiring direct platform integration.

---

### ⚠️ Advisory Boundary

This module supports structured investigation and decision-support workflows.

It does not:

- Provide investment advice
- Generate trading signals
- Recommend financial decisions
- Execute transactions

AI contributes structured interpretation and alternative perspectives. Investigation, interpretation, and decision-making remain the responsibility of the user.
