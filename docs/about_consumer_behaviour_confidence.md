This module examines household consumption trends, retail performance, and behavioural indicators of consumer sentiment.
It integrates consumption volume metrics, sales turnover, and confidence signals (where available) to evaluate national
demand-side dynamics.

Rather than explaining behavioural outcomes or prescribing demand models, the module highlights shifts in consumer
momentum, psychological posture, and retail resilience across macroeconomic phases.

It supports comparative evaluation across countries or regions with adequate consumption and sentiment data, and integrates
seamlessly with the broader Financial Insight Tools ecosystem.

---

- **Consumption Signal Framing**: Surfaces volume, trend, and growth dynamics of household and retail demand.
- **Sentiment & Confidence Context**: Frames consumer posture through composite indices and expectation metrics.
- **Behavioural Layers**: Includes optional indicators such as saving intentions, durable goods planning, and survey-based mood indices.
- **Modular Integration**: Compatible with other thematic modules and downstream AI or observational workflows.
