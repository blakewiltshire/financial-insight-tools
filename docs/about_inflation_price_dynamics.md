This module explores inflationary signals and pricing behaviour across the economy. It presents headline and core inflation
metrics, alongside optional granular components such as housing, energy, and producer prices.

Rather than attributing cause or forecasting inflation regimes, the module highlights pricing pressure directionality,
component contributions, and structural inflation characteristics.

It is deployable across any country with consistent consumer and producer price series, and integrates directly with downstream
policy framing, AI-enhanced diagnostics, or observation-layer tools.

---

- **Inflation Signal Framing**: Tracks headline and core inflation trends, including acceleration, volatility, and contribution mix.
- **Component-Level Insight**: Enables evaluation of major drivers like food, energy, housing, and services.
- **Structural & Cyclical Context**: Frames inflation in terms of supply-demand pressures, pass-through dynamics, or base effects.
- **Modular Integration**: Fully compatible with other thematic and observational modules within the Financial Insight Tools suite.
