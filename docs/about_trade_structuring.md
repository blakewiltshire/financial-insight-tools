### Key Functions

- **Trade Configuration Tools**: Supports both directional and spread-based structuring
- **Capital Allocation Logic**: Incorporates leverage, capital at risk, and reward/risk metrics
- **Spread & Pairs Calculators**: Calculates spread ratios, Z-scores, and weighting alignment
- **Interactive Planning Dashboard**: Captures, edits, and stores trade drafts within the module
- **Reference Journal Upload**: Allows optional import of external watchlists or strategic idea sets
