## Intermarket & Correlation

The **Intermarket & Correlation** module explores structural relationships across macroeconomic indicators, asset classes, thematic signals, macro transmissions and positioning. It supports cross-domain analysis by quantifying co-movement, spread dynamics, and interdependencies — enabling system-level insight into asset interaction patterns.

This launcher provides unified access to the suite’s analytical tools:

- **Cross-Asset Correlation:** Measure co-movement between macro variables, equities, indices, and other asset classes
- **Spread & Ratio Insights:** Analyse pair relationships, relative strength, and valuation reversion across dual-asset structures
- **Correlation Heatmaps & Themes:** Visualise multidimensional correlation matrices and identify dominant macro themes
- **Relative Macro Transmission:** Track exogenous differentials across systems, including growth, rates, spreads, FX pressure, and macro-asset divergence
- **Positioning & Crowding:** Review leveraged positioning, crowding, percentile extremes, and flip behaviour across core futures markets

### ⚠️ No investment advice or recommendations are provided

This module supports structured planning and diagnostic exploration. Interpretive judgement, scenario assumptions, and execution decisions remain the responsibility of the end user. No trading signals or investment recommendations are produced by this system.
