## Intermarket & Correlation

The **Intermarket & Correlation** module explores structural relationships across macroeconomic indicators, asset classes, and thematic signals. It supports cross-domain analysis by quantifying co-movement, spread dynamics, and interdependencies — enabling system-level insight into asset interaction patterns.

This launcher provides unified access to the suite’s analytical tools:

- 🔀 **Cross-Asset Correlation:** Measure co-movement between macro variables, equities, indices, and other asset classes
- ⚖️ **Spread & Ratio Insights:** Analyse pair relationships, relative strength, and valuation reversion across dual-asset structures
- �️ **Correlation Heatmaps & Themes:** Visualise multidimensional correlation matrices and identify dominant macro themes
